Implementations

Thanks to many of Asura users, there are multiple implementations of the template for easy integration with back-end frameworks. The following are some of them:

  • Persian RTL by Mohammad Sharifi
  • Yii 2 by Tobias Munk
  • Laravel by Arjay Angeles
  • Laravel 5 package by Sergi Tur Badenas
  • Laravel-Asura by Jeroen Noten
  • Symfony by Marc Bach
  • Rails gems: asura2-rails by Nicolas Besnard and lte-rails (using Asura sources) by Carlos at RacketLogger
  • CoPilot (Asura with Vue.js) byGil Ferreira

Note: these implementations are not supported by Aris Ripandi. However, they do provide a good example of how to integrate Asura into different frameworks. For the latest release of Asura, please visit our repository or website
